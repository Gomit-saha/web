   Dosa

   Dosa is a popular South Indian thin crepe made with fermented rice and lentil batter. It is served with chutney, sambar, and potato masala. Making dosa is easy once you have the batter ready. You can make the batter at home    or buy it from a store.Here are the steps to make dosa at home:

   Ingredients :

  •    1.Maida - 1 cup
  •    2.Salt to taste
  •    3.Baking powder – 1/4 tsp
  •    4.Oil – 2 tsp
  •    5.Garlic cloves – 6/7 (chopped)
  •    6.Onion – 1 (chopped)
  •    7.Cabbage – 1/4 bowl (shredded)
  •    8.Capsicum – 1/4 bowl (shredded)
  •    9.Carrot – 1/4 bowl (shredded)
  •    10.Soya sauce – 1/2 tsp
  •    11.Red chili sauce – 1/2 tsp
  •    12.Chili paste – 1/2 tsp
  •    13.Tomato ketchup – 1/2 tsp

   Method :

  •    Step 1: Mix maida, 1/4th tsp salt, baking powder and knead into a soft dough.
  •    Step 2: In a pan add oil and fry garlic and onion. Add all vegetables and cook for 2/3 min.
  •    Step 3: Add soya sauce, red chili sauce, chili paste, tomato ketchup and salt and cook for another minute.
  •    Step 4: Make small balls of dough and roll into thin circles and fill a spoon of filling in each.
  •    Step 5: Steam for 10 minutes in a steamer.
  •    Step 6: Serve hot with red chili sauce.. Enjoy!
